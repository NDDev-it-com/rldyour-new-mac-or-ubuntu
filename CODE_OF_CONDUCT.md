# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Empathy and kindness toward each other.
- Respectful discussion, even in disagreement.
- Constructive feedback.
- Owning mistakes and learning from them.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, threats, and abuse.
- Sexualized behavior or unwanted advances.
- Personal attacks.
- Public sharing of private information without permission.
- Any conduct reasonably considered unprofessional.

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing these standards. Reports are handled
through the contact listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). All reports are reviewed
promptly and treated seriously.

## Enforcement

Participants asked to stop harmful behavior must comply. Repeated or severe violations
may result in temporary or permanent removal from project spaces.
